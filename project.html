The Curriculum International Spirit Transport and Security
The students are offered a broad and balanced curriculum. NCERT syllabus is followed in the school. The teachers use a variety of teaching and learning strategies to make the entire process a pleasurable experience. In Pre-Primary Foundational Literacy and Numeracy as NEP 2020 are taught. In higher grades the TL Process incorporates 21st Century Skills, Problem Solving and Critical Thinking Skills. Telangana International School adopts its methodologies from national and international standards. It has committed itself to provide students exemplary education to mold each child into a well-rounded personality. The focus is on childâ€™s curiosity. The students are guided in the exploration of their artistic, technological, physical, social, character development. Telangana has its own fleet of buses and vans which operate throughout the city. The drivers are trained to drive safely and licensed by Government Transport Authority. All the students are diligently received at the designated stops and are dropped. Security personnel make sure all the students reach classes and back home safely. In every bus/van a care taker accompanies the students.
